## Identity & Voice

**Logo:** Stacked wordmark — "MEGA" (navy), "DISCOUNT" reversed inside a red bar, "APPLIANCES" (navy). Punchy, retail/circular sale energy. The condensed bold typography is the brand's strongest visual signature.

**Voice cues from existing copy:**
- "Satisfy your cravings promptly" — friendly, slightly informal
- "Get lasting protection" — reassurance + value
- "Real savings, honest guidance, dependable service" — small-business, family-business feel
- Mixes premium-product copy ("Café-quality espresso", "advanced range hoods") with deal-circular value claims

**Positioning:** The store is *not* a pure scratch-and-dent depot. It carries a curated mix of premium specialty brands (Waterdrop, Robam, Seewin, Casabrews) plus mainstream large appliances (KitchenAid refrigerators, washers, dryers, ranges). The brand sits between "premium showroom" and "discount outlet" — call it **the Houston neighborhood premium-discount appliance dealer.**

## Service Promises (3 pillars)

1. **Fast fulfillment** — same-day pickup or weekly delivery
2. **On-site service** — professional installation/service started within 72 hours
3. **Real warranty** — 1-year free standard, upgradeable up to 4 years

These are the strongest assets in their copy. They will become the homepage value-prop strip.
