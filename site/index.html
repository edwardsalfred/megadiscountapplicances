Frequently Asked

Answers before you call.

Most calls we get are about delivery, warranty, and how the open-box pricing actually works. Here's the short version.

How does the warranty work?
Every appliance ships with a free 1-year warranty covering parts and labor. You can upgrade to a 4-year extended warranty at checkout — just ask, or mention it on the phone when you call to confirm stock.
Are these scratch-and-dent? Will my appliance look used?
Most of our inventory is new, open-box, or display-floor units with cosmetic imperfections. The vast majority of "scratches" are in spots that disappear once the appliance is installed — the side of a fridge against a cabinet, the back of a washer, the corner of a dishwasher under the counter. We're upfront about every blemish before you buy, and we test every unit before it leaves the showroom.
Do you deliver and install?
Yes. Same-day pickup is available at the showroom, weekly delivery routes cover Greater Houston, and we serve Dallas–Fort Worth on a separate schedule. Professional on-site installation is available within 72 hours of purchase for most jobs — call us to schedule.
Do you offer financing?
Yes — call the showroom to discuss available financing partners and approval options. Most credit profiles qualify for some kind of payment plan.
What's your return policy?
See our Refund & Returns Policy for the full terms. The short version: returns are evaluated case-by-case; defective units are covered by the warranty; cosmetic complaints on disclosed open-box items aren't returnable.
Can you confirm what's actually in stock today?
The featured deals on the website turn over fast. The fastest way to confirm a specific unit is in stock today is to call (346) 730-4870 — we'll check the floor in real time.
